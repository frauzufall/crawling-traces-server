Crawling Traces - Plaint Our Wall

Participants:

HOW TO

Grab your smartphone and open this URL: frauzufall.de/wand.

You can now draw an our wall by wiping the screen of your smartphone. You will see drawing lines as a projection on our wall in the video stream.

There can be a time telay up to more than one minute. This is why you will also see digital lines as a preview of what you and other participants are drawing.

No information about your connection will be stored, you can draw completely anonymous. You can also open multiple tabs or reload the page on your phone to draw another line in a different color.
